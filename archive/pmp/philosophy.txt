**Practice makes perfect (PMP)** projects to build durable knowledge

Goals of PMP:

- understand the basic building blocks of domain
- grow our knowledge from a base of understanding the simple building blocks
- effectively move knowledge from short-term to long-term memory

Ingredients of PMP project:

- simple repetitive task that is easily repeatable
- documentation/specification/reading resource targeted at task
- short periods of time spaced over several days
- a though-out next step

Why PMP works:
utilizes effective learning techniques

- retrieval practice
- spaced repitition
